Our story

Founded by maker-designer A. Redes, our studio started as a one-room workshop with a simple promise: to make enduring furniture that brings warmth and personality to homes. Over the years we've collaborated with local artisans, experimented with sustainable finishes, and developed a careful sourcing network that respects both people and place.

We believe in transparency, and try to make every step of the creative process clear — from the forest to the finished piece.
